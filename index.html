Overview

I was introduced to this official MoMA dataset in the Interactive Data Visualization class as part of MS Data Analysis and Visualization. Intrigued by the access to this massive 151,834 works of art from their collection, I decided to focus on gender and nationalities in order to explore and understand the MoMA collection dataset.
